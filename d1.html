Cilantro-Celery Juice Punch

Ingredients

1 piece (1-inch; about 1/2 ounce) fresh ginger

1 bunch (large; about 4 ounces) cilantro

1 (about 8 ounces) Granny Smith apple, halved, cored and cut into wedges

8 (about 12 ounces) celery ribs

2 teaspoons lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Directions

Step 1

In an electric juicer, juice the ginger,
cilantro, apple and celery. Stir in the lemon juice and serve right away.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 182 calories; protein 1g; carbohydrates 28g; fiber 1g; sodium 8mg.

Prep : 5 mins | Total : 10 mins

Servings : 2 Glasses
