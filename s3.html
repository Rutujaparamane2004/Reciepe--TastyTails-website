Mutton Keema Samosa Recipe

Directions

  • Step 1 :

    • To begin making Croquettes Recipe With Bhetki Fish, for the fish stuffing, in a mixing bowl, add the fish steaks, quartered onions, crushed garlic cloves, bay leaves, crushed green cardamom, cinnamon, a generous sprinkle of salt and about a cup of water to a pressure cooker.

  • Step 2 :

    • Cook over a high heat, one whistle I would reckon on.

  • Step 3 :

    • Allow the pressure to be released by itself. Once it cools down on its own, carefully debone the fish steaks, and mash the fish with a wooden masher or ladle. Keep aside till needed.

  • Step 4 :

    • Heat about 1 tablespoon oil in a pan, once hot, add the chopped onions and garlic, fry on medium high heat till close to caramelised.

  • Step 5 :

    • Add the chopped green chillies, saute for a minute or two.

  • Step 6 :

    • Add the mashed fish, tomato ketchup, salt and sugar.

  • Step 7 :

    • Cook on a low heat, stirring occasionally, for 5-7 minutes or until combined well enough. Switch off and allow to cool till luke warm so that it is easy to handle with bare hands.

    Ingredients

    5 Bhetki fish , (Or sea bass steaks)

    1 Onion , quartered

    2 cloves Garlic , crushed

    2 cloves Garlic , finely chopped

    3 Green Chillies , finely chopped

    2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds

    tablespoon Tomato Ketchup

    1 tablespoon Oil

    2 Onion , finely chopped

    2 Bay leaves (tej patta)

    1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini)

    Salt , to taste

    For the potato cases

    3 Potatoes (Aloo) , boiled and mashed

    1 teaspoon Black pepper powder

    For assembling and frying the croquettes

    1 Whole Egg , whipped

    tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida)

    1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs , (or more) for coating Oil , for frying

  • Step 8 :

    • Form 10 oval roundels and keep aside.

    Assembling and frying the Fish Croquettes

  • Step 9 :

    • Sprinkle a little salt and black pepper to the mashed potatoes and mix well till combined.

  • Step 10 :

    • Divide the potatoes into 10 equal portions.

  • Step 11 :

    • Take one portion of mashed potato in the palm of your hand, make a well in the center.

  • Step 12 :

    • Place a portion of the fish filling in the potato well, bring the corners of the potato case together and cover the fish stuffing. (It should be a very thin coating of mashed potato over the fish stuffing)

  • Step 13 :

    • Gently shape into elongated croquettes. Keep a plate of flour, a bowl of whisked eggs, and a plate of bread crumbs are ready,

  • Step 14 :

    • Roll the croquettes over flour, and dip into whipped egg and roll over breadcrumbs.

  • Step 15 :

    • Heat oil in deep frying pan, once hot, deep fry till golden

Equipments Used :

Hard Anodized Pressure Cooker, Hard Anodised Deep-Fry Pan, Glass Mixing Bowl

Prep : 15 mins | Cook : 40 mins | Total : 60 mins

Servings : 5-6
